## Why "Sustainable" Isn't Enough Anymore
In 2026, the fashion industry has shifted focus to **Regenerative Organic Certified (ROC)** standards. Unlike standard organic cotton, regenerative farming actually sequesters carbon back into the soil, turning the apparel industry into a potential solution for climate change rather than a contributor.

### Key Benefits of Regenerative Fiber
1. **Carbon Sequestration**: Actively draws CO2 from the atmosphere.
2. **Biodiversity**: Restores local ecosystems and insect populations.
3. **Soil Health**: Eliminates the need for synthetic fertilizers, preserving water tables.
