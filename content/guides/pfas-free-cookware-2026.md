## Frequently Asked Questions
### What does "PFAS-Free" actually mean in 2026?
It means the product is verified to contain 0% PTFE, PFOA, PFOS, or any of the 9,000+ synthetic chemicals in the PFAS family. Ethos verifies this by cross-referencing third-party lab reports from companies like SGS.

### Why avoid traditional non-stick?
Traditional coatings often break down at temperatures above 500°F (260°C), releasing polymer fumes that can cause "Teflon Flu" and environmental contamination that persists for centuries.
