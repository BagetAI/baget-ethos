## What makes tech "Sustainable" in 2026?
1. **Material Origin**: Post-consumer recycled plastics and ocean-bound minerals.
2. **Repairability**: Modular designs that allow for battery replacement.
3. **End-of-Life**: Clear take-back programs or compostable components.
